Contemporary Tz'utujil

Modern Tz'utujil face both opportunities and challenges from tourism at Lake Atitl√°n, one of Guatemala's most visited destinations. Tourism provides economic opportunities through crafts, guiding, and hospitality, but also brings cultural commodification pressures and rising land prices. The Tz'utujil navigate these pressures while maintaining traditions in one of the world's most visually stunning indigenous homelands.

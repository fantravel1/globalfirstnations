Who Are the Bengali?

Partition and Independence

Bengal's 1947 Partition divided the region between India (West Bengal, Hindu majority) and Pakistan (East Bengal/East Pakistan, Muslim majority). The division severed cultural unity, creating refugee crises and lasting trauma.

Contemporary Kiowa

Modern Kiowa in Oklahoma pursue cultural preservation while addressing economic challenges. The tribe has limited gaming (Kiowa Casino) and faces poverty rates common to Oklahoma tribes. Language preservation is criticalâ€”Kiowa has approximately 1,000 speakers, declining but more viable than many Plains languages. Cultural programs maintain the Gourd Dance and other traditions. Kiowa artists continue the Six's legacy; the Kiowa Black Legging Society and other warrior societies remain active. How the Kiowa sustain their artistic traditions, preserve language, and build economic capacity shapes this principal people's future on the southern Plains.

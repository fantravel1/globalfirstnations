Mapuche-Pehuenche People

The Pehuenche (approximately 10,000-15,000) are a Mapuche subgroup inhabiting the Andean volcanic regions of Chile and Argentina at 1,000-2,500 meters. They live among active volcanoes including Lonquimay and Llaima, enduring earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, ash fall, and harsh mountain winters.
